2. BBA Marketing
What is BBA Marketing?
BBA Marketing focuses on promoting products and services to customers effectively. It helps students understand consumer behavior, branding, advertising, and market strategies used by companies.
What you study in BBA Marketing
Marketing Management and Consumer Behavior
Students learn how customers think, what influences buying decisions, and how products are positioned in the market.
Advertising, Sales, and Digital Marketing
These subjects explain promotion techniques, online marketing, social media, and sales strategies used by modern businesses.
Skills you gain
Communication and persuasion skills
Students learn how to present ideas, influence customers, and build strong brand messages.
Market analysis and creativity
They develop the ability to analyze trends and create innovative marketing strategies.
Career options after BBA Marketing
Marketing Executive / Brand Manager
They plan and execute marketing campaigns for products and services.
Digital Marketing Specialist / Sales Manager
They work in online marketing, social media, and sales growth roles.

3. BBA Finance
What is BBA Finance?
BBA Finance focuses on money management, investments, banking, and financial planning. It prepares students to handle financial decisions in businesses and organizations.
What you study in BBA Finance
Financial Management and Accounting
Students learn how to manage funds, budgets, profits, and losses.
Investment Analysis and Banking
These subjects explain stock markets, financial instruments, and banking systems.
Skills you gain
Numerical and analytical skills
Students develop strong abilities in calculations and financial analysis.
Risk assessment and decision-making
They learn to evaluate financial risks and returns.
Career options after BBA Finance
Financial Analyst / Accounts Executive
They analyze financial data and prepare reports.
Banking and Investment roles
They work in banks, finance firms, and investment companies.
